## ✨ Key Features

- **30+ Expert-Level Templates**: Curated prompts covering Coding, Data Analysis, Creative Writing, Planning, and Learning.
- **Dynamic Favorites System**: Save your most-used templates locally. Favorited items are persisted in your browser's `localStorage`.
- **Adaptive UI**: A seamless **Dark/Light Mode** toggle to suit your working environment.
- **Lightning Fast Filtering**: Instant search and category-based filtering using high-performance JavaScript.
- **One-Click Copy**: Integrated clipboard functionality with visual success feedback.
- **True Responsiveness**: Optimized for everything from ultra-wide monitors to small mobile viewports.
- **Syntax Highlighting**: Visual cues within templates (e.g., `<span class="ph">[PLACEHOLDERS]</span>`) to make customization intuitive.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Flexbox & Grid), and ES6+ JavaScript.
- **Typography**: [Syne](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Syne), [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono), and [Instrument Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Instrument+Sans).
- **Icons**: Emoji-based and CSS-styled primitives for maximum performance and zero external dependencies.
- **Persistence**: Browser `localStorage` API.
- **Hosting**: GitHub Pages.

## 📖 How to Use

1.  **Browse & Search**: Use the navigation pills to filter by category or use the search bar for specific keywords.
2.  **Star Your Favorites**: Click the ★ icon on any card to save it to your "Favorites" tab.
3.  **Copy & Customize**: Click the "Copy" button. The template text is instantly added to your clipboard.
4.  **Prompt Claude**: Paste the template into Claude (or your LLM of choice) and replace the `[BRACKETS]` with your specific data.
